Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in the PicToPDF app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from you. All images and PDFs created are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

- Storage: To access images from your gallery and save the generated PDFs.

Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob may collect information about you to serve personalized ads. For more information, please see Google's Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
