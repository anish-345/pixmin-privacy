Privacy Policy for NexPlayer
Effective Date: February 14, 2026

NexPlayer ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect
NexPlayer is designed to be a privacy-first application. Most processing occurs directly on your device.

Media Files: We require the READ_MEDIA_VIDEO and READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE permissions to identify, organize, and play video files stored locally on your device.
Device Information: We may collect technical information such as device model, OS version, and unique device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID) for performance monitoring and advertising purposes.
Usage Data: We may collect anonymous data on how you interact with the App (e.g., features used, playback duration) to improve our services.
2. On-Device AI Processing (Private by Design)
NexPlayer features advanced AI-powered subtitle generation.

Local Transcription: All audio extraction and AI transcription (using Whisper/Sherpa-ONNX) are performed entirely on your device.
No Cloud Uploads: Your video and audio data are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party cloud for transcription. All media remains on your device at all times.
3. Permissions and Use
We only request permissions that are essential for the App’s functionality:

Storage/Media: To browse and play your local videos.
Internet: To download the AI models during initial setup and to serve advertisements via third-party providers.
Advertising ID: Used by ad networks to provide relevant advertisements (see Section 4).
4. Third-Party Services & Advertising
Our App may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you:

Google AdMob: We use AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use your device’s Advertising ID to show personalized ads based on your interests. You can reset or opt-out of this via your Android device settings.
Firebase/Analytics: We may use analytics tools to help us understand App performance and stability (crash reports).
5. Data Retention
Generated Subtitles: Subtitles generated by the AI are stored locally in your App's private cache. You can manually delete this data at any time via the "Cache Management" section in the App settings.
Personal Data: We do not store personal identification data on our servers.
6. Children's Privacy
NexPlayer does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
