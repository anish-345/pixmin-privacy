Pixmin Privacy Policy

Your privacy, protected by design

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Overview

Pixmin ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application, Pixmin (the "App").

Our Core Principle: We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information or images. All processing happens on your device.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our services:

✓ What We Collect:

  • Device Permissions: Camera, Gallery, and Storage access (only when you grant permission)
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics about app usage (via AppMetrica)
  • Advertising Data: Ad networks may collect device information for ad personalization

✗ What We DO NOT Collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Biometric data
  • Any other sensitive personal data

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only for:

  • Enable core app functionality (image compression, format conversion, PDF generation)
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Processing

All image processing happens on your device:
  • Image compression is performed locally
  • Format conversion is performed locally
  • PDF generation is performed locally
  • No images are uploaded to our servers
  • No images are shared with third parties

5. Data Storage

Processed images are stored locally on your device:

  • Location: /storage/emulated/0/Pictures/Pixmin/
  • You have full control over these files
  • You can delete files at any time
  • Uninstalling the app removes all associated data

7. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

Permission Purpose
CAMERA To take photos directly in the app
READ_MEDIA_IMAGES To access your photo gallery
READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE To access images on your device (Android 12 and below)
WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE To save processed images (Android 10 and below)
INTERNET For ads and analytics

You can revoke any permission at any time through your device settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Pixmin does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed to be safe for all ages. We do not require user accounts or collect personal information from any user, regardless of age.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • All processing happens on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No user accounts or authentication required
  • No sensitive data collection

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke app permissions at any time
  • Delete all app data
  • Uninstall the app
  • Request information about what data we collect (minimal)
  • Opt out of personalized ads

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Your continued use of the app constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
